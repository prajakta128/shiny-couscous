üìã When to Call Emergency Services

  • Call 112 or 102 immediately if:
    • Someone is unconscious or unresponsive
    • Severe chest pain or difficulty breathing
    • Severe bleeding or poisoning
    • Suspected stroke (facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
    • Severe allergic reactions
    • Accidents or injuries
  • Have information ready:
    • Your current location or address
    • Patient's age and medical conditions
    • Any medications they're taking
    • Contact information
  • Stay calm and:
    • Follow dispatcher instructions carefully
    • Keep the line open
    • Provide accurate information
    • Do not hang up until told to do so
